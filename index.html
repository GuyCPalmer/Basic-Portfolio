About Me

Guy Palmer is the Tequila Avion Brand Director based in Austin, TX where he is responsible for defining and executing consumer and trade events throughout the area, and spearheading local sales efforts for the award winning “world’s best tequila.” Instrumental in the brands success, Guy directs the growth of Avion for the world’s fastest growing premium tequila.


Guy earned his degree from the University of Texas in 2005. He enjoys reading, traveling, and experiencing new cultures, but likes to stay active within his home community. In his down time, you can usually find him running the board walk along town lake, fishing in South Texas or exploring the vast Texas Hill Country.
